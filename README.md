# python-challenge

This challenge analyzes two different csv files.

The PyBank challenge looks at financial data to look over the profits and losses over a period of 86 months. The total months, net total profits, average change per month, greatest increase and decrease were all calculated. 

The PyPoll challenge analyzes the results of an election. Total votes were calculated as well as the percentage and total votes each candidate earned. The winner was also named. 

Both files have comments on how the data was analyzed.
